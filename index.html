About

House Protocals CPT is a division of House Collective Entertainment, a popular and trendy group of music artists who tour South Africa in search of House Music addicts. At House Protocals CPT, we focus on showcasing the talent of local South African DJ's while offering an experience of once in a lifetime - unless you attend our events again of course. Our aim is to uplift the local artists who has the talent but lacks the clout. Most importantly, our main focus is to make sure that House Music lovers like you and I regularly feel those vibrations running down our spine. So what are you waiting for? Check out our services below and start booking before the tickets run out!

Services

Below are some priceless captured memories. Hover over them to discover our services!

Helping Local DJ's

We are always on the lookout for new, talented local DJ's so if this sounds like you then keep reading. We help these DJ's get a fair chance at leaving their mark in the people's hearts and their tunes in the people's memories. If you are a local DJ who feels like you could make it big one day, contact us and you can audition. Every DJ deserves a fair chance!

Clubs and Events

We frequent various clubs and host events in multiple different locations all around South Africa. We are always looking for enthusiastic people who love House Music to join the crowd! So if this sounds like you, don't be shy to reach out and find the upcoming events and clubs that we'll be performing at! We'll keep a close eye out for you.

Drinks and Refreshments

Partying for hours makes you thirsty doesn't it? Well we've got you covered by taking care of your drinks and refreshments so that you don't have to leave the venue and miss out on any exciting memories!

Sheesha and Chill

Not the party type? Are you just here because your friends wanted you to come? Or does House Music just wanna make you chill and relax? No worries, because we make sure you can comfortably relax in our lounges while enjoying some Sheesha with a flavor of your choice!

The Main DJ

Meet Mpho Joe Bothloko, the owner of this whole operation. Of course the party can't be a party without this guy's music right? Well that's no problem for us because he'll be at every event we host, playing the grooviest House Music your ears have ever tasted!

Bookings and Reservations

Looking for something more private? Well we've got you covered there as well! We are available for bookings and reservations, simply just reach out explaining the date, time and agenda adn we'll be sure to reply with an answer!
